About me

I am a first year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at NYU Tandon School of Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Ramesh Karri and Dr. Shaloo Rakheja. I received my B.Tech. and M.Tech. in Electrical Engineering from the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras(IITM) in 2017. For the period of 2016-2018 I worked in Prof. Anjan Chakravorty's group at IITM on numerical modelling of electron transport in a Silicon Nanowire Transistor(SNWT) both in the quantum and semi-classical domains. I also investigated the effects of self-heating and the noise associated with electron transport in nanoscale devices.
