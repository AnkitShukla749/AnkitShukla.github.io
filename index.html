About me

I graduated with Dual Degrees (B.Tech. and M.Tech.) in Electrical Engineering from the department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras(IITM) in 2017. Currently, I am a Project Associate in Dr. Anjan Chakravorty's group at IITM where I am involved in modelling self-heating in a Silicon Nanowire Transistor(SNWT). Prior to this I was a member in the same group and worked on modelling electron transport in a SNWT using NEGF and BTE.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Device Modelling

  • Computational Modelling

  • Self-Heating in devices

  • Nano-electronics
